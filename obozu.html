Unarmored Defense.

While the obozu is wearing no armor and wielding no shield, its AC includes its Wisdom modifier.

Unarmored Movement.

While the obozu is wearing no armor and wielding no shield, its speed increases by 20 feet (included in its speed).

Stunning Strike (3/Day).

The obozu can interfere with the flow of ki in an opponentâ€™s body. When the obozu hits another creature with a melee weapon attack, it can attempt a stunning strike. The target must succeed on a Constitution saving throw (DC 13) or be stunned until the end of its next turn.

Grappler.

The obozu has advantage on attack rolls against a creature it is grappling. It can use its action to try to pin a creature grappled by it. To do so, the obozu makes another grapple check. If it succeeds, the obozu and the creature are both restrained until the grapple ends.

Step of the Wind.

The obozu can take the Disengage or Dash action as a bonus action on its turn.

Innate Spellcasting.

The obozu's innate spellcasting ability is Wisdom (spell save DC 13, +5 to hit with spell attacks). The obozu can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:

At will: detect thoughts, disguise self, magic missile


3/day each: charm person, detect magic, invisibility, suggestion

1/day each: blade barrier, blink

Shielded Mind.

The obozu is immune to scrying and to any effect that would sense its emotions, read its thoughts, or detect its location.
